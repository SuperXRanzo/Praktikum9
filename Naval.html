NAVAL COMBAT TIPS

Ship Positioning

Always angle your ship to reduce the chance of enemy shells penetrating your armor. Presenting your broadside makes you an easy target for enemy gunners.

Use islands and terrain features to conceal your movements and protect portions of your vessel from enemy fire.

Gunnery Skills

Target enemy vessels at their waterline where armor is typically weaker. Successful hits in these areas can cause flooding and critical damage.

For larger ships, aim for ammunition storage areas or engine rooms to maximize damage potential.

Torpedo Tactics

Use torpedoes strategically by launching them in corridors where enemy ships are likely to pass or during close engagements.

Remember that torpedo detection is difficult in rough seas or poor visibility conditions. Use this to your advantage!
