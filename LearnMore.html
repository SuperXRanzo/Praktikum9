ABOUT WAR THUNDER

War Thunder is a free-to-play, cross-platform, massively multiplayer military game dedicated to combat aircraft, armored vehicles, and naval craft from the early 20th century to the modern era.

Developed by Gaijin Entertainment, War Thunder puts you in control of the most iconic military vehicles from World War II through the Cold War and beyond. With stunning graphics, realistic physics, and historically accurate details, War Thunder offers an unparalleled combat experience.

GAME FEATURES

Realistic Combat

Experience the most authentic combat simulator with detailed damage models, realistic ballistics, and historically accurate vehicles. Every machine gun, cannon shell, and torpedo behaves according to its real-world counterpart.

Massive Vehicle Selection

Choose from over 2,000 meticulously detailed aircraft, tanks, helicopters, and ships from the major combat theaters of the 20th century. Each vehicle is reconstructed with precision based on historical documents.

Multiple Game Modes

Play casual in Arcade mode with simplified flight and driving mechanics, challenge yourself in the more realistic Realistic Battles, or test your skills in the unforgiving Simulator Battles with full real-world physics.

GAME MODES

Arcade Battles

Fast-paced action with simplified flight and physics models. Perfect for beginners and casual gameplay sessions. Features include markers for enemies, simplified damage models, and unlimited ammunition.

  • Fast-paced gameplay
  • Enemy markers visible
  • Simplified controls
  • Respawns available

Realistic Battles

A balance between accessibility and realism. More authentic physics and damage models, limited ammunition, and fewer visual aids. The most popular mode among War Thunder players.

  • Realistic physics
  • Limited enemy markers
  • Historical matchmaking
  • Limited ammunition

Simulator Battles

The most realistic experience possible. Full simulation physics, no visual aids or enemy markers, limited views, and first-person-only perspective. For veterans seeking the ultimate challenge.

  • Full simulation physics
  • No enemy markers
  • First-person view only
  • Real cockpit controls

DEVELOPMENT HISTORY

2012

War Thunder announced and entered closed beta testing with only aircraft available.

2013

Official release and launch on Steam. The game featured primarily air combat with over 200 aircraft.

2014

Ground Forces expansion released, introducing tanks and other armored vehicles to the game.

2016

Introduction of new nations including Italy and France, expanding the vehicle roster significantly.

2018

Naval Forces update launched, adding ships and naval combat to War Thunder's combined arms warfare.

2020

New Engine introduced with significantly improved graphics, lighting, and physics systems.

2022

Modern combat expansion featuring contemporary military vehicles and advanced weaponry.

2024

War Thunder continues to expand with new vehicles, nations, and gameplay features.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Yes, War Thunder is completely free to play. All vehicles, maps, and game modes are accessible without spending any money. Premium features are available for purchase to accelerate progression or add exclusive vehicles, but they're not necessary to enjoy the full game experience.

Minimum Requirements:
  • OS: Windows 7/8/10
  • Processor: 2.2 GHz
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: DirectX 10.1 compatible with 512 MB VRAM
  • Storage: 17 GB available space
Recommended Requirements:
  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5 or better
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: DirectX 11 compatible with 2 GB VRAM
  • Storage: 17 GB available space

You earn research points (RP) and Silver Lions (SL) by participating in battles. Research points unlock new vehicles, while Silver Lions are used to purchase them and pay for repairs and ammunition. Progression is organized in tiers, with each nation having its own research tree. Complete matches, accomplish objectives, and destroy enemy vehicles to progress faster.

Premium accounts provide boosted research points and Silver Lions earnings (typically +50%), access to premium missions and events, and additional in-game bonuses. Premium accounts are time-limited subscriptions that help players progress faster through the research trees, but don't provide any combat advantages in battles themselves.

Yes, War Thunder is available on PlayStation, Xbox, and PC with cross-platform play enabled. You can play with friends regardless of their platform, and your account progress can be transferred between platforms (with some restrictions for console accounts).
